Wuhan Pandemic

Outbreak

There is no evidences yet that would proof what was the real beginning of COVID-19. Even tough exists few theories with many constituencies.

One of them is that the virus broke out might be related to some laboratory. More widely recognized version is theory that says about wet market in Wuham where people sells exclusive and wild animals, dead and alive for private purposes, and from one of them virus jumped onto human patient zero.

However, virus in a short time was infecting further people. Due to international flights, and that the China is the center of many worldwide corporations and the biggest world exporter - not so long time after beginning in China virus started to spread over other countries.

Virus

In most cases, new viruses evolve from old viruses. The chance of a virus evolving successfully infect a new species is extremely low but there are over seven billions people on the planet, and most of them interact with animals everyday even as eating food.

Coronaviruses are a family of viruses that cause disease in animals. Seven, including the new virus, can affect to humans, but most just cause cold-like symptoms. The one which presently causes the biggest damage, is named Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) and is more dangerous than its predecessors.

Governments answers

Respectively to the informations how catching is virus, in many countries state authorities started reacting immediately to prevent spreading. Ones of the most important are:

  • Organise new points to help infected people, like build new hospitals in 10 days, that happened in China!
  • Short time after first cases outside PRC, flights between particular countries started being cancelled and the first one was China - USA.
  • Many governments try to keep citizens at homes as far as possible. They ban mass meetings including public events. Lot of them were suspended or postponed, such as Chinese New Year festival or concerts for which many tickets were sold already.

Shopping

In cause of the fear from the infection, people started buying large amount of products. This led to situations where in shops were deficities of products on the shelfs and buyers were waiting before opening.

Over time this problem was normalized. Even so, in many countries long queues are forming anyway. That happens because of restrictions about number of clients in the time in certain shops. Mainly, bigger shopping centres are completely closed.

Major of companies loses fortune when people stay in their homes, but not all. Businessess like these producing face masks increases sales up to 500%!

Recovery

It is now known that probability of recover is different depending on age. Media reports that the elder age is higher death rate, however keep in mind that people in very young age also have low immune system.

Statistic show that mortality rate for people between 75-84 years old is about 4,5%, but for age over 84 years olds death ratio amounts 13.5%! That's why they should take care most of all.
