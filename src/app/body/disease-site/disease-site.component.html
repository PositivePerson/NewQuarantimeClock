Symptoms

Coronavirus can affect different ways on different people. It's good to know symptoms of infection demonstrating.

Common symptoms:

  • fever
  • tiredness
  • dry cough

Less frequently but may occur:

  • nasal congestion
  • aches and pains
  • diarrhea
  • runny nose
  • sore throat

If some not listed occur you should find out more in the internet or consult with your medical provider.

Hygiene

It is especially important to keep high level of hygiene these times. You can use an alcohol-based hand sanitiser if your hands are not visibly dirty. Ensure you wash your hands or use a hand sanitiser before:

  • entering area where are other people
  • touching thing used by other people
  • putting on gloves or mask

And after:

  • using bathroom
  • coughing or sneezing
  • removing mask or gloves
  • touching any objects which are in continuous use by others

Suspicion

If you think you might have COVID-19, you must take extra care. In most cases sick person can recover at home without medical care. Monitor your symptoms. Most common are fever and cough.

If there are other housemates, wear mask at home, too. Use different crockery, cutlery and disinfect frequently used places / objects everyday. If possible, use separate bathroom too.

Observe your symptoms. Coronavirus behaves similarly to flu. Stay self-isolate untill tests confirm that you are healthy. Usually it takes 1-2 days .

Do not go to the clinic, call 911!

Treatment

There are few things to get to know about being infected. In most cases no medical cure is needed. Take care of yourself. Get rest and stay hydrated. That usually helps.

Signs which says there is need to get medical attention immediately:

  • Trouble with breathing
  • Continuous pain or pressure in the chest
  • Bluish lips or face

Above symptoms are not all of them. If you have other and you suspect you might have COVID-19, you should consult your doctor.

Until recovery you should stay separate from others. If it is not possible and you must be around other people or animals, even at home, take extra care. It's recommended to wear mask and If there are opportunity, use separate bathroom too.
