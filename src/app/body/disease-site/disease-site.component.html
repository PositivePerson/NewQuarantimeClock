Shopping

In cause of the fear from the infection, people started buying large amount of products. This led to situations where in shops were lacks / deficities of products on the shelfs and buyers were waiting before opening.

Over time this problem was normalized. Even so, in many countries long queues are forming anyway. That happens because of restrictions about number of clients in the time in certain shops. Mainly, bigger shopping centres are completely closed.

Major of companies loses fortune when people stay in their homes, but not all. Businessess like these producing face masks increases sales up to 500%!

Recovery

It is now known that probability of recover is different depending on age. Media reports that the elder age is higher death rate, however keep in mind that people in very young age also have low immune system.

Statistic show that mortality rate for people between 75-84 years old is about 4,5%, but for age over 84 years olds death ratio amounts 13.5%! That's why they should take care most of all.
